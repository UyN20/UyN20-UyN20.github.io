Best show this year.

So far an array of different shows came out and I watch many of them. But at of all of them there is usually the best out of the others. The one that has an amazing plot, great acting, etc. In my opinion, I think that the tv show "Scandal" on "ABC" was the best this year. It had high stakes with plenty of twists and turns for the characters. It also left a lot of potential for it's next season. Sadly, the next season, season 7, will be it's last. Hopefully they make it better than all of the others.
