Interests

I love many different things but most of all, I love tv, movies, and comics. I like Marvel, Disney, Star Wars, DC comics, and others. Here are some of my favorites and preferences.

DCTV shows

I love to watch all of the DCTV shows and my favorite ones are on the channel "The CW". I love to rewatch both "Supergirl" and "Legends of Tomorrow". It's fun to watch how the characters react to one another and I think that the plot is more interesting and crazy in the latest seasons.

Marvel tv shows

I also love to watch most of the Marvel tv shows in both the channel "ABC" and in "Netflix". My favorites to watch is "Agents of SHIELD" and "Jessica Jones". The cast in both shows are amazing in portraying their characters. If they're in the comics, they are loyal to the character but if not, they're performance was still amazing. The ending in both shows leave me wondering "How could this be? How could this happen?" I really can't wait for the next seasons for both shows.

Other tv

I also like to watch other non-comic book shows. For example, I like to watch "Scandal" on "ABC" and "Baby Daddy" on "Freeform". They're very interesting and are funny as well.

Movies

I don't just love tv, I love movies as well. I like to watch a lot of sci-fi movies. I like to watch movies for example "Star Wars" and "DC" movies. I would also watch animation movies from "Disney", "DreamWords", and more. I could never choose the best movie because each are very different and makes them distinct from all of the other ones.
